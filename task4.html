About us

The K S R Institute for Engineering and Technology is another landmark of the Aarthi Educational & Charitable Trust which has the perquisite of sprawling education proportionally to the society. It has been founded in the year 2011 by the man of great insight, Thiru.R. Srinivasan. He is the younger son of Lion Dr.K.S.Rangasamy whose altruism enlightens the society. His constant dreams and endeavors erected the dome of knowledge for quenching the long unanswered quest among the blooming minds. The embellishment of the college holds the new doctrine of teaching, will be ideal for students to kindle the dormant spirit towards learning. The college is an optimal place for pedagogical as well as ethical values.
